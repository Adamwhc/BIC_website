Coronavirus (COVID-19)


BIC and ACHBM campuses are still open. this week we have transition you to online learning: This week for Elicos and after 20 April for VET. wherever we can. If you remain enrolled, your Confirmation of Enrolment (COE) will not be impacted by studying online. If your lecturer advises you that there are on-campus requirements, or you still want to come on to campus to access resources, you can continue to do so. The health and well-being of students and staff is always our priority so, if you are feeling unwell please take care of yourself and do not come to campus. Intake for VET ACHBM will be on 20 April 2020.
