Mascot Campus (Main Campus)


BIC English & ACHBM is conveniently located in central Mascot within short walking distance from Mascot train station and buses. Here, students have easy access to many restaurants, state of the art facilities, accommodation and Sydney airport. At only a short 8-minute train ride from Mascot station into the heart of Sydney, students can easily commute to campus or visit famous hotspots around Sydney.


Library

ACHBM provides students with a comfortable environment to achieve their goals with our well-equipped library and study space which offers access to high-speed Internet and air-conditioning. Students have the option to stay on campus to study or consult with our welcoming trainers.

Access our library to browse through a large variety of resources/texts available to rent or use including:

  • Books, magazines, DVDs;
  • WI-FI
  • Photocopying and writing services
  • Individual and group study facilities
  • Refrigerator & microwave
  • Vending Machine

Mascot Library is only a short walk away located at 2 Hatfield Road, Mascot. Students can access a range of daily newspapers, magazines, multi-lingual publications, reference books and a range of other books and publications. Membership is free.

The famous Green Square Library is also only one stop away by train (355 Botany Rd, Zetland NSW 2017)


Support Services


BIC English and ACHBM is committed to supporting students through their academic journey. Our student administrative, support and learning support services are designed to ensure that students can receive responses in a timely manner and have a positive learning environment and experience throughout their studies.
