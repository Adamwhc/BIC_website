Foundation English 2(FE2)

CRICOS CODE: 095904G


  • Who is this course for?


    This course is also called General Purpose English and is designed for anyone who would like to improve their English! It will help your English in a general way and covers English that is useful in everyday life. The course covers all areas of English: speaking, listening, writing and reading.


  • Level


    Intermediate, Upper-Intermediate and Advanced


  • Duration


    Up to 35 weeks


  • Intake Dates


    Every Monday Morning except on Public Holidays and School Holidays


  • Hours per week


    Hours per week 20 hours face to face
    PLUS Access to self-study area and Computer labs


  • Assessment


    Regular tests for eligibility for higher level.


  • Entry Requirements


    Minimum age of entry is 18 years old.
    Students will be given a placement test at Orientation.


  • Campus Delivered (according to PRISMS)


    Mascot, Bondi, Darwin
